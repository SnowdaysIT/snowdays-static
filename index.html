There are no days like Snowdays!

This was the motto for the very first Snowdays in 2003, when approximately 100 people ruled the slopes of Obereggen. Since then more than 10 years have passed. While the event thrived and progressed from year to year, many thrilling experiences were made: this led to an incredible expansion of our event. Snowdays attracts students from all over the world to come to South Tyrol to take part in our wintersport event, the biggest one organised by students for students in Europe.

Even after all these years the passion of the organisers to offer to the participants an unforgettable weekend in South Tyrol is still strong. Enthusiasm and teamwork are the leading features that drive the SCUB-Team (Sports Club University Bolzano), which over the last months has already intensively started the organisation of the 15th edition of the Snowdays.

Our goals are to provide three unforgettable days of sport, joy and entertainment. During the event students from different universities and countries get to know each other and the companies that support the event, while enjoying together lots of different activities and exploring South Tyrol.

EVENTS

Snowdays is the biggest winter sports event organised for students by students in Europe. From the 5th until the 7th of March SCUB organises sporting competitions, entertainment and parties in Bozen and surroundings.

The organising committee consists solely of volunteers, enthusiastic about sports, snow and mountains. Involved are students from the varying faculties of the UniBz, which is supporting the event.
